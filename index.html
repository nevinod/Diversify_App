The purpose of Diversify! is to help cryptocurrency investors diversify their holdings. Most coins are highly correlated so it is difficult to hedge. When you enter a coin into Diversify!, the app will find the coin in the top 100 that is most negatively correlated with yours, helping you diversify your holdings. Please enter the coin's symbol into the input box (all caps), hit enter, and the app will suggest a coin, and its symbol will appear in the key above the graph next to the maroon box.
